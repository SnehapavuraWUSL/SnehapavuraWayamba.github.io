Our Projects

We started our carrier,the “Sneha Pavura” on 11th of December 2017.
We have successfully completed three projects so far

First Project

Nakkawaththa Ruggassagara Junior School Project 

Our very first project was renovating two buildings, including the library of Ruggasagara Kanishta Vidyalaya. Our fund provided the school students with sports and chess equipment. We gave those children the basic knowledge of information technology. We donated books for the school library. We also conducted a motivational programme for the students at our university. Through that programme we taught them how to face exams and their future lives successfully. The end of the motivational programme was marked with a fire night.

Second Project

Scouting Experience

Our second project was creating and handing over a brand-new scouting troop for the Ruggasagara Kanishta Vidyalaya. We gave a teacher and the students with the required knowledge. At the end of this project we had a scout badge investiture programme

Third Project

English & IT skill Development Project

Our third project was conducting English language enhancement classes at the Kanadulla pirivena in Kuliyapitiya. Our aim was to provide the children with adequate knowledge on English language. We were able to satisfyingly complete this programme with an awarding ceremony on the 27/10/2019 at the Kanadulla pirivena. It should be stated that this will be a continuous project conducted by our fund.
